PR Data Submission

We’re collecting data for PR research. Use this page to upload screenshots of your PR Research Results to help out the data gathering. Keep in mind:

  • If you do not remember the name of the project, do not guess. Pick “I don’t know.” We can try to figure it out anyway.
  • Try your best to post all of your research results, or at least whenever you remember, regardless of content. If you only upload “interesting” results then our data collection will be skewed.
  • You won’t be able to see the images you uploaded, but there is a check for exact duplicates. If you don’t remember whether or not you uploaded a screenshot, just upload it again.
  • Upload screenshots as-is. Cropping them makes it harder to auto-detect what is there.
